Things to know

Located on the north eastern side of Banks Peninsula, approximately an hour and twenty minutes’ drive from Christchurch. We will help facilitate transport for those traveling from afar.

  • Plenty of parking along the waterfront
  • Waterfront camping ground
  • No shops nor cellphone coverage, however, both can be found with a drive if need be

Accommodation

We have secured an exclusive waterfront paddock for you to pitch a tent or set up your glamping station. There are toilet blocks and showers can be found if the ocean doesn’t cut the mustard.

If camping is not your scene we have secured a few baches. We do have secured most baches and if there are beds available, after our allocation we will send out some notification if there are spare beds available.
